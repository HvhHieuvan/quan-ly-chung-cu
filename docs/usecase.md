# Use Case He Thong Quan Ly Chung Cu

## 1. Tac nhan
- Quan tri vien
- Nhan vien quan ly
- Cu dan

## 2. Chuc nang cua Quan tri vien
- Dang nhap he thong
- Quan ly tai khoan
- Quan ly can ho
- Quan ly cu dan
- Quan ly hop dong
- Quan ly hoa don
- Quan ly thong bao
- Xem bao cao thong ke

## 3. Chuc nang cua Nhan vien quan ly
- Dang nhap he thong
- Quan ly thong tin cu dan
- Lap hoa don dich vu
- Xac nhan thanh toan
- Tiep nhan phan anh
- Gui thong bao

## 4. Chuc nang cua Cu dan
- Dang nhap he thong
- Xem thong tin can ho
- Xem hoa don
- Thanh toan hoa don
- Gui phan anh
- Xem thong bao

## 5. Mo ta tong quat
He thong quan ly chung cu giup quan ly thong tin can ho, cu dan, hoa don, thanh toan, phan anh va thong bao mot cach tap trung, chinh xac va de theo doi.
